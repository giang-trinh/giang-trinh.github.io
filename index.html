I am currrently a lecturer and researcher at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology. I have been a postdoctoral researcher at the LIRICA team within the LIS laboratory from 04/2022 to 09/2024 and a postdoctoral researcher at the LIFEWARE team within INRIA Saclay from 10/2024 to 08/2025. I defended my PhD at the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology in November 2021.

Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of theoretical computer science, symbolic artificial intelligence, and computational systems biology. I focus on formal frameworks such as Boolean networks, Petri nets, answer set programming, and abstract argumentation, exploring their interconnections and their applications to the modeling, analysis, and control of biological systems.
